<objective>
Deliver the RES-08 sensitive-class failover smoke test — `scripts/integration-smoke/smoke-sensitive-failover.py` + its `sensitive-failover-report-schema.json`. The smoke is the black-box proof that Phase 3's RES-08 machinery holds end-to-end against the deployed gateway: a `data_class: sensitive` tenant request, during an induced upstream failure, **queues or fails closed** (503), an `audit_log` row records the decision (`upstream='blocked_sensitive'`), zero `audit_log_content` rows are written, and the request is **NEVER proxied to OpenAI/OpenRouter**.

This implements the SC1 verification artifact for INT-03 (Telefonia sensitive call-audio) and the sensitive half of INT-04 (Cobranças sensitive). It composes two analogs: the `smoke-chat-ifix.py` script skeleton (CLI, config, report-write, exit-code contract) and the `sensitive_block_test.go` integration test (what to assert).

Purpose: every prior phase shipped a smoke-script + exit-code + schema-validated-report contract for its integration; Phase 9's sensitive integration needs the same, and it is the load-bearing evidence that the LGPD never-external guarantee actually works.
Output: `scripts/integration-smoke/smoke-sensitive-failover.py`, `scripts/integration-smoke/sensitive-failover-report-schema.json`.
</objective>
