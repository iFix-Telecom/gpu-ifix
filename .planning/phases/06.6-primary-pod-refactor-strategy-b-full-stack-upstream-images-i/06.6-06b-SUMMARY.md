## Decisions Made

- **sync.RWMutex over atomic.Pointer[string] for Scraper.url** (reviews #13, Codex LOW). Less invasive: existing `scrape()` reads `s.url` as a plain field. Switching to `atomic.Pointer` would force `.Load()` at every site, rippling through tests and any future call sites. RWMutex preserves the read pattern (RLock around access, Lock around `SetURL`). Rationale documented in the `Scraper` struct comment and in the `SetURL` method doc.
- **Empty URL semantics for Scraper.SetURL.** `SetURL("")` causes `scrape()` to short-circuit *without* counting a failure (no increment of `consecutiveFail`, no `vramUnknown` flip). This is the contract the Phase 6.6 primary lifecycle relies on for the `StateReady → StateAsleep` transition — `primary.Reconciler.markAsleep` will call `SetURL("")` to stop scraping a non-existent pod, and the scraper goroutine must idle quietly instead of spamming failures.
- **Count is delegation, not a new counter source.** Adding a parallel counter would have introduced two sources of truth; instead `Count` is a 1-line wrapper that documents the semantic intent at the drain-polling call site (`evaluateDraining`) while reusing the existing race-tested `GlobalInflight` path.
- **Updated TestOverrideTier0_NonExistentRole to use "vision".** The existing test used `"stt"` as a non-existent role to assert silent no-op semantics. After Task 1 made `"stt"` a valid override key, the test would no longer exercise the no-op path. Switched to `"vision"` (genuinely absent from the v6.6 map) and kept the assertion that `stt`'s base URL is untouched — preserves the original intent while compatible with the new 3-role map.
