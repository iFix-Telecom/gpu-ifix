## Phase Boundary

Restaura tier-0 local-stt (Speaches/faster-whisper-large-v3 no pod primary) E adiciona cascade tier-1 com 3 upstreams ordenados por `tier_priority`:

1. **gemini-stt** (tier-1 prio=10) — primary fallback ($0.05/h batch)
